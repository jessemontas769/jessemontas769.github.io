The term "training" comes from the English word training, meaning exercise. For a long time, this meaning was put into the concept of "sports training", understanding by this term the repeated performance of a sports exercise in order to achieve the highest result.
Gradually, the content of the concept of "sports training" has expanded and is now understood as a planned pedagogical process, including training an athlete in sports technique and tactics and the development of his physical abilities.
